2024

James had a late oppurtunity to participate in the 2024 Ginetta Junior Championship, where he will race across many UK tracks such as Silverstone, Donington Park, Oulton Park and Snetterton. Due to have no pre-season testing James had little time to adjust to the car before the first round, however he has learned quickly and by the second round already scored two 4th place finishes across the weekend.
